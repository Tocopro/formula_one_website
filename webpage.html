BASIC FORMULA ONE RACING
As Told by Nicholas

MLA Citation : Media.Formula1.com URL (https://media.formula1.com/image/upload/ content/dam/fom-website/manual/Misc/2021-Master-Folder /F1%202021%20LAUNCH%20RENDERING%20(2).jpg. transform/9col/image.jpg) Date Accessed: 06/19/2023

Formula One Motorsport is dominated by Ferrari and Mercedes Benz teams. But recently the Red bull team Has been taking the first finish position in many races. One thing I have noticed over the few racing seasons Recently was that if a team won some race or races, they would tend to dominate and win almost all the Races for that season and even several seasons. Now some fans would probably think the driver has a good racing car that season(s) or the drivers Skills have been improving and now the results are proof of that. I would like to think formula one racing Is a sport of strategy or plan more than a driver’s skills or car. I acknowledge those are very important In winning races obviously but the word formula one comes with rules which are designed to place all the Participating teams on an even plane. There is unspoken fact that there is black magic involved in placing the cars at an added advantage over the other Competitors. It is aerodynamics. The difference between a few seconds lead comes down to how the car can manage Aerodynamics forces without compromising performance. Engines, suspensions, Tires are also very important in a car’s performance and handling. But the dark art of designing aerodynamics To give an edge over other drivers is the determinant which driver has a lead or overall season performance Is the greatest. And here comes the unspoken truth about all these, there is cheating or bending rules but done in ways that formula one scouts Or other teams cannot detect easily. The team with the smartest aerodynamic cheat will certainly dominate the race.
